Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Kolay Bul Ekspres ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Site.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Search and Filter Usage: When you search for products, apply filters, or sort by price, we may log these queries locally to improve your experience.
  • Comments and Reviews: If you submit reviews or comments, we collect the content you provide.
  • Affiliate Redirect Data: When you click on affiliate links, we may track that a redirect occurred (product ID, timestamp) for performance monitoring.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and click patterns are recorded by our web server.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use session cookies for CSRF protection and essential site functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including device model, operating system, and mobile network data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our Site and services
  • To process and track affiliate redirects for commission and performance reporting
  • To display personalized product recommendations and search results
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience

4. Data Retention

We retain personal and usage data only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Log data and click records are typically retained for 90 days. Session cookies expire when you close your browser. You can clear cookies manually via your browser settings.

5. No Sign-Up Required

Kolay Bul Ekspres does not require user registration or personal information to browse products. You can view all product listings, prices, and details without creating an account or providing email, name, or personal data.

If you choose to submit a review or comment, you may optionally provide a name; however, this is voluntary and not required.

6. Third-Party Links and Affiliate Partners

Our Site contains links to third-party websites and affiliate partners (eBay, AliExpress, Amazon, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites before providing your information.

When you click an affiliate link and are redirected to a partner site, your data transmission is governed by that partner's privacy policy, not ours.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Our Site uses HTTPS encryption to protect data in transit. We do not store credit card or sensitive payment information.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of non-essential tracking by disabling cookies in your browser settings.
  • Data Portability: You may request your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete such information promptly. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated Privacy Policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification if we collect email addresses in the future

Your continued use of the Site after any modifications constitute your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: support@kolaybulexpres.com

Website: https://www.kolaybulexpres.com/

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days. For GDPR and data protection inquiries, please include "Data Privacy Request" in the subject line.

Summary

In short: Kolay Bul Ekspres respects your privacy. We do not require registration, do not sell your data, do not use intrusive tracking, and provide transparent information about our practices. You can browse our Site freely without providing personal information.
