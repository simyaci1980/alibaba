Privacy Policy

1. Our Approach

Kolay Bul Ekspres is a product discovery website. We do not require sign-up, and we do not ask visitors for sensitive personal information just to browse products.

This page explains what limited information may still be processed for security, functionality, and affiliate link operations.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require account creation to view products.
  • We do not ask for payment card information on this website.
  • We do not sell personal data to third parties.

3. Limited Data We May Process

Even without user sign-up, some technical data can be processed automatically during normal website operation:

  • Server logs: IP address, browser type, date/time, and requested pages.
  • Security/essential cookies: Used for core protection features (for example, CSRF/session safety).
  • Affiliate click events: When a visitor clicks a store button, we may store basic event records such as product ID, store, and timestamp.

4. Why We Process This Data

  • To keep the website secure and stable
  • To operate product redirections correctly
  • To understand basic site performance (for example, broken links or failed redirects)
  • To improve product listing quality and user experience

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell visitor data. We do not share private visitor information for advertising resale.

When you click an affiliate link, you leave our site and continue on a partner platform (such as eBay, AliExpress, or Amazon). At that point, the partner's own privacy policy applies.

6. Data Retention and Protection

We keep only the minimum records needed for technical operations and service quality. Records are kept for a limited period and protected with reasonable security practices.

The site is served over HTTPS to protect data in transit.

7. Your Choices

  • You can browse without creating an account.
  • You can disable cookies from your browser settings (some security-related features may be affected).
  • You can stop using the site at any time.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us:

Email: support@kolaybulexpres.com

Website: https://www.kolaybulexpres.com/

Quick Summary

Short version: You can use this site without account registration. We only process limited technical and operational data needed to run the service safely, and we do not sell visitor data.
